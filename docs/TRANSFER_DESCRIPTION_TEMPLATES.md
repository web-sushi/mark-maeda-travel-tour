# Sample Transfer Description Templates

Copy these templates into your Admin form Description field.
Replace the placeholder text with your actual transfer details.

## Template 1: Narita Airport to Tokyo

```markdown
## About This Transfer

Experience comfortable and reliable private transfer service from Narita Airport to central Tokyo. Our professional drivers ensure a smooth journey to your hotel or accommodation.

## What's Included

- Professional English-speaking driver
- Meet & greet at arrivals hall with name sign
- Flight tracking included (no extra charge for delays)
- All tolls and highway fees
- Bottled water
- Child seats available upon request
- Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup

## Pickup Process

1. **Collect Your Luggage** - After clearing customs, collect your bags from baggage claim
2. **Find Your Driver** - Proceed to the arrivals hall and look for your driver holding a sign with your name
3. **Luggage Assistance** - Driver will assist with your luggage and guide you to the vehicle
4. **Enjoy Your Ride** - Relax and enjoy your comfortable ride to your destination

## Estimated Travel Time

Typical journey times from Narita Airport:

- **Tokyo Station:** 60-90 minutes
- **Shinjuku:** 90-120 minutes
- **Shibuya:** 90-120 minutes
- **Roppongi:** 90-120 minutes
- **Ginza:** 70-100 minutes

*Note: Times may vary depending on traffic conditions and time of day.*

## Tokyo Drop-Off Zones

We provide door-to-door service to all Tokyo locations including:

**Central Tokyo**
- Chiyoda, Chuo, Minato wards
- Tokyo Station & Marunouchi
- Imperial Palace area

**Shopping & Entertainment**
- Shinjuku & Shibuya
- Harajuku & Omotesando
- Ginza & Roppongi

**Traditional Areas**
- Ueno & Asakusa
- Akihabara
- Yanaka & Nezu

**Bay Area**
- Odaiba
- Tokyo Bay waterfront

## Important Notes

> **Flight Information Required:** Please provide your flight number when booking. We'll track your arrival time and adjust pickup accordingly if your flight is delayed.

**Advance Booking Recommended:** For hotel to airport transfers, please book at least 24 hours in advance to ensure vehicle availability.

**Luggage Capacity:** Standard luggage allowance is included. For oversized items (ski equipment, golf bags, etc.), please contact us after booking.

**Contact After Booking:** For any special requests or questions, use the "Manage Booking" page to reach us directly.
```

## Template 3: Tokyo to Mt. Fuji / Hakone

```markdown
## About This Transfer

Scenic private transfer from Tokyo to Mt. Fuji and Hakone region. Travel in comfort while enjoying views of Japan's countryside and iconic mountain.

## What's Included

- Experienced driver familiar with mountain roads
- Hotel to hotel door-to-door service
- All tolls including expressway fees
- Complimentary bottled water
- Luggage handling assistance
- Flexible departure time

## Pickup Process

Your driver will arrive at your Tokyo hotel at your scheduled time:

1. Driver calls/texts 15 minutes before arrival
2. Meet in hotel lobby
3. Driver assists with luggage loading
4. Begin your scenic journey

## Estimated Travel Time

From Tokyo to:

- **Hakone Yumoto:** 2-2.5 hours
- **Lake Kawaguchi (Fuji Five Lakes):** 2-2.5 hours
- **Gotemba:** 2-3 hours
- **Fujinomiya:** 2.5-3 hours

*Times are estimates via expressway. May vary based on traffic and weather.*

## Route Options

**Via Tomei Expressway** (Fastest)
- Direct route to Hakone/Gotemba area
- Scenic mountain views

**Via Chuo Expressway** (Fuji Five Lakes)
- Best for Lake Kawaguchi area
- Views of Mt. Fuji (weather permitting)

Your driver will recommend the best route based on your destination and traffic conditions.

## Important Notes

> **Weather Advisory:** Mt. Fuji and Hakone areas can experience snow and fog, especially November-March. Please allow extra time during winter months.

**Stop Requests:** If you'd like to stop for photos or restroom breaks, just let your driver know. We recommend the Ashigara Service Area for its excellent views.

**Luggage:** Standard luggage is included. Ski/snowboard equipment welcomed (please mention in booking notes).
```

## Template 5: Tokyo Disney Resort Transfer

```markdown
## About This Transfer

Magical start to your Disney adventure! Private transfer service from central Tokyo to Tokyo Disney Resort (Tokyo Disneyland / Tokyo DisneySea).

## What's Included

- Family-friendly driver
- Direct door-to-door service

2. Meet in lobby with family
3. Child seats pre-installed if requested

- **Shinjuku:** 45-60 minutes
- **Shibuya:** 45-60 minutes

**Tokyo Disneyland**
- Main entrance drop-off area
- Disney Ambassador Hotel
- Tokyo Disneyland Hotel

**Tokyo DisneySea**
- Main entrance drop-off area
- Hotel MiraCosta
- Tokyo DisneySea Hotel

**Disney Resort Area**
- Official Disney hotels
- Partner hotels
- Good Neighbor hotels

## Important Notes

> **Child Safety Seats:** Please specify children's ages at booking. We provide appropriate seats free of charge (infant seat, convertible seat, booster seat).

**Park Opening Rush:** During park opening hours (typically 8:00-9:00 AM), traffic around Disney can be heavy. We recommend early departure from your hotel.

**Return Flexibility:** Return transfers can be flexible within your booking window. Contact driver via phone/app when you're ready to leave (2-3 hours notice appreciated during busy days).
```

## Template 6: Ski Resort Transfer (Nagano/Niigata)

```markdown
## About This Transfer

Mountain adventure private transfer from Tokyo to popular ski resorts in Nagano and Niigata prefectures. Travel comfortably with your ski/snowboard gear.

## What's Included

- Driver experienced with mountain/winter driving
- Vehicle equipped with winter tires & chains
- Spacious cargo area for ski/snowboard equipment
- All expressway tolls
- Bottled water & light snacks
- Flexible departure scheduling
- Hotel to hotel service

## Pickup & Drop-Off

**From Tokyo:**
Pick up from your Tokyo hotel, apartment, or Shinkansen station

**To Ski Resorts:**
Drop off at your resort hotel entrance or nearby designated point

**Return Transfer:**
Reverse journey back to Tokyo

## Estimated Travel Time

From Tokyo to:

- **Hakuba Valley Resorts:** 4-5 hours
- **Nozawa Onsen:** 3.5-4.5 hours
- **Shiga Kogen:** 4-5 hours
- **Myoko Kogen:** 4-5 hours
- **Naeba/Kagura:** 3-4 hours
- **Gala Yuzawa:** 2-2.5 hours

*Times are estimates via expressway. Winter conditions may require extra time.*

## Equipment & Luggage

**Ski/Snowboard Gear:**
- Skis/snowboards with bags
- Ski boots in separate bags
- Helmets and accessories

**Regular Luggage:**
- Suitcases and bags
- Backpacks

**Note:** Please specify number of ski sets and luggage pieces when booking so we can arrange the appropriate vehicle.

## Winter Travel Tips

Our drivers are experienced with winter mountain driving and equipped for snow/ice conditions.

**Service Areas:**
We recommend stopping at:
- Yokokawa SA (near Gunma) - Last major stop before mountains
- Various local rest areas for restroom breaks

**Weather Monitoring:**
Your driver monitors road conditions and weather forecasts. In extreme weather, route adjustments may be necessary for safety.

## Important Notes

> **Winter Equipment Required:** All vehicles have winter tires. Chains carried as backup for severe snow. Your safety is our priority.

**Departure Timing:** For same-day ski access, we recommend departure by 7:00 AM from Tokyo. This allows arrival by resort opening time.

**Return Booking:** For return transfers, please book at least 24 hours in advance. Flexible return dates can be confirmed closer to your travel date.
```
